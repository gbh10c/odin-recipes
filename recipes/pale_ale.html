Pale Ale

Description

This is a classic American Pale Ale brewed with Michigan-grown malt and hops, and fermeted with a traditional American ale yeast.

Ingredients

  • 3.15 gal Water
  • 3 lb 14 oz 2-Row Malt
  • 5 oz Caramel 40L Malt
  • 0.35 oz Michigan Copper Hops @30 min
  • 0.2 oz Michigan Copper Hops @15 min
  • 0.25 oz Michigan Copper Hops @whirlpool
  • 1 pkg Safale US-05 American Ale Yeast

Steps

  1. Mash grains at 152 degrees for 60 minutes
  2. Mash out at 170 degrees for 10 minutes
  3. Boil wort for 60 minutes and follow hop schedule above
  4. Cool wort to 66 degrees and transfer to sanitized fermenter
  5. Aerate word and pitch yeast
  6. Ferment for 10-14 days at 68 degrees
  7. After reaching terminal gravity, transfer to keg or bottles, carbonate, and serve
