Hazy

Description

This is a modern Hazy Pale Ale featuring massive late-hop additions of juicy Michigan varietals, supported by wheat and rye malts.

Ingredients

  • 3.32 gal Water
  • 4 lb 2-Row Malt
  • 5 oz White Wheat Malt
  • 5 oz Rye Malt
  • 0.4 oz Michigan Triumph Hops @0 min
  • 2 oz Michigan Copper Hops @4-day dry hop
  • 1.6 oz Michigan Triumph Hops @4-day dry hop
  • 1 pkg Safale S-04 British Ale Yeast

Steps

  1. Mash grains at 152 degrees for 60 minutes
  2. Mash out at 170 degrees for 10 minutes
  3. Boil wort for 60 minutes and follow hop schedule above
  4. Cool wort to 66 degrees and transfer to sanitized fermenter
  5. Aerate word and pitch yeast
  6. Ferment for 7-10 days at 68 degrees
  7. After reaching terminal gravity, add dry hops for 4 days
  8. At completion of dry hop, transfer to keg or bottles, carbonate, and serve
