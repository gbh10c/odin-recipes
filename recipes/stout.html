Stout

Description

This is a tropical stout brewed with loads of roasty and caramel malts along with fresh, fruit-forward hops.

Ingredients

  • 3.4 gal Water
  • 4 lb 12 oz Maris Otter Malt
  • 3 oz Chocolate Malt
  • 3 oz Chocolate Rye Malt
  • 3 oz Caramel 10L Malt
  • 3 oz Caramel 80L Malt
  • 3 oz Caramel 120L Malt
  • 3 oz Special B Malt
  • 0.4 oz Michigan Copper Hops @60 min
  • 0.4 oz Michigan Copper Hops @10 min
  • 0.4 oz Michigan Copper Hops @0 min
  • 1 pkg Safale S-04 British Ale Yeast

Steps

  1. Mash grains at 154 degrees for 60 minutes
  2. Mash out at 170 degrees for 10 minutes
  3. Boil wort for 60 minutes and follow hop schedule above
  4. Cool wort to 66 degrees and transfer to sanitized fermenter
  5. Aerate word and pitch yeast
  6. Ferment for 10-14 days at 68 degrees
  7. After reaching terminal gravity, transfer to keg or bottles, carbonate, and serve
